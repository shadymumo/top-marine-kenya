Our History

Topmarine Shipping Company (K) Ltd core business is handling and moving any type of containerized cargo moving from China to Kenya. We deliver cost effective solutions focusing on individual customer requirements. We recognize that an end to end supply chain solution is one that reduces costs whilst improving client service and providing visibility throughout the entire logistics service. Our efficient services are designed to meet the basics of our clientele demands not only ion maritime sector but also hauling sea cargo, air cargo, sourcing, procurement consultancy and warehousing. In partnership with maritime bodies we are engaged in multifarious activities with large portfolio cargo concessions, shipments survey and inspection, pre-shipment inspection, geological services, legal advisory services, marine and non-marine consulting services, feasibility studies, ism code (international safety management code), and collateral management services inclusive. Whether you require distribution or fulfillment, defined freight forwarding, or a complete supply chain solution, Topmarine Shipping Company (K) Ltd can provide you with a customized solution tailored to your needs. We can provide an end to end supply chain solution including Freight Forwarding and Customs Brokerage services, Project Management for the logistical movement of out-of-gauge and abnormal cargo, the handling and movement of bulk minerals, fresh produce, automotive, palatalized goods and Transport and Distribution in and out of Kenya. We regard ourselves as partners in supply chain solutions with a professional approach on operational excellence with a customer centric attitude.
