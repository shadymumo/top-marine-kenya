What is cross-border e-Commerce?

Are you looking for new markets for your business? Cross-border e-Commerce is the general term for purchases made online from businesses in other countries or jurisdictions. It involves either buying via websites in other countries or through third-party “marketplaces” (such as India’s Flipcart and and Snapdeal) that host international merchants.
